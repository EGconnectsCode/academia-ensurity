Limitations & Exclusions GuidesGuías de Limitaciones y Exclusiones

Exact eligibility from AHS Agent Sales Guide (05/2026) · All applicants must legally reside in the United StatesElegibilidad exacta de la Guía del Agente AHS (05/2026) · Todos los solicitantes deben residir legalmente en los EE.UU.

⏱️
Short Term Medical (STM)
PPO Standard, PPO Guaranteed Issue, Legacy (MD/OR)
✅ Elegibilidad — Edades de Inscripción (Agent Sales Guide p.17)
  • Primary: 60 days – 64 yrs and 10 months
  • Spouse/domestic partner: 14 yrs – 64 yrs and 10 months
  • Dependent child: 60 days – 25 yrs (IL: through 29)
  • Age coverage ends: Primary & Spouse: 65 yrs · Dependent: 26 yrs
  • Standard Issue: Requires health questionnaire
  • Guaranteed Issue: No health questionnaire — all accepted
  • Available in: AL, AR, AZ, FL, GA, IA, ID, IL, IN, KS, KY, LA, MI, MO, MS, MT, NC, ND, NE, NV, OH, OK, SC, SD, TN, TX, UT, VA, WI, WV, WY
  • Legacy (MD, OR): Aetna Open Choice PPO — same benefit in/out-of-network
⏳ Waiting Periods / Períodos de Espera
  • Next-day effective dates: injury & preventive care available right away
  • Sickness benefits: 7-day wait (waived if application sign date >7 days from effective date)
  • Idaho rewrite limit: 63-day gap required between AHS STM plans
  • TX/NC: duration limited to 3+1 months
🚫 Main Exclusions / Exclusiones Principales
  • Pre-existing conditions: diabetes, cancer, heart disease, stroke, mental health, substance use disorders
  • Prescription drugs (may not be covered depending on plan)
  • Maternity care · Preventive screenings · Pediatric care
  • Does NOT satisfy ACA Minimum Essential Coverage (MEC)
  • May have NO limit on out-of-pocket costs (unlike ACA plans)
  • NOT available in: CA, MA, NJ, NY, VT and other restricted states
🚩 Red Flags — Client may NOT qualify
  • Pregnant or planning pregnancy — maternity NOT covered
  • Needs coverage for existing chronic conditions
  • Cancellation (full refund): within 10 days for STM · 30 days for supplemental
🚑
Accident Fixed-Benefit
Guaranteed Issue · ~46 states
✅ Elegibilidad (Agent Sales Guide p.17)
  • Primary: 18 yrs – 64 yrs
  • Spouse/domestic partner: 18 yrs – 64 yrs
  • Dependent child: Birth – 25 yrs (IL: through 29)
  • Age coverage ends: Primary & Spouse: 70 yrs · Dependent: 26 yrs
  • Coverage ends at age 65 in: FL, GA, ID, MA, OH, VA
  • Guaranteed Issue: NO health questionnaire required
  • Outline of coverage required at point of sale in: AK, AL, AR, AZ, CA, CO, CT, DE, DC, ID, IA, IL, IN, HI, KS, KY, LA, ME, MI, MO, MS, MT, NC, ND, NE, NH, NJ, NM, NV, OK, OR, PA, RI, SC, SD, TN, TX, UT, WV, WI, WY
⏳ Waiting Periods
  • No waiting period — coverage from effective date
  • Many benefits require treatment within 30 days of accident
🚫 Main Exclusions
  • Illness or disease — accident-only coverage
  • Self-inflicted injuries, illegal activities, war, active military duty
  • Under influence of alcohol or drugs
  • Workers' Compensation covered injuries excluded
  • Reinstatement: Unlimited timeframe · Guaranteed Issue — via phone
🚩 Red Flags
  • Client needs illness coverage — accident plans do NOT cover illness
  • Benefits require treatment within 30 days of accident — remind client to file claim promptly
🚑
AcciMED (AME + AD&D)
Requires health questionnaire
✅ Elegibilidad (Agent Sales Guide p.17)
  • Primary: 18 yrs – 64 yrs
  • Spouse/domestic partner: 18 yrs – 64 yrs
  • Dependent child: Birth – 25 yrs (IL: through 29)
  • Age coverage ends: 65 yrs / 65 yrs / 26 yrs
  • Requires health questionnaire
  • Available through Preferred level LIFE Association membership
🚫 Key Limitations
  • Multiple policies NOT allowed
  • Requires new application for reinstatement (cannot reinstate via phone)
  • Primary insured must reapply for coverage to include all requested dependents (removal)
  • Benefits require treatment within 30 days of accident
🚑
TrioMED (AME + CI + AD&D)
Requires health questionnaire
✅ Elegibilidad (Agent Sales Guide p.17)
  • Primary (AME): 18 yrs – 64 yrs · ends 65 yrs
  • Primary (CI): 18 yrs – 64 yrs · ends 70 yrs
  • Spouse/domestic partner: 18 yrs – 64 yrs
  • Dependent child: Birth – 25 yrs (IL: through 29) · ends 26 yrs
  • Requires health questionnaire
  • Available through Preferred level LIFE Association membership
🚫 Key Limitations
  • Multiple policies NOT allowed
  • Benefits require treatment within 30 days of accident
  • CI: 3 categories of serious illnesses covered
🛡️
VitalGuard (AD&D)
Guaranteed Issue · 9 benefit levels $50K–$300K
✅ Elegibilidad (Agent Sales Guide p.17)
  • Primary: 18 yrs – 64 yrs · coverage ends 70 yrs
  • Spouse/domestic partner: 18 yrs – 64 yrs · coverage ends 70 yrs
  • Dependent child: Birth – 25 yrs · coverage ends 26 yrs
  • Guaranteed Issue: NO health questionnaire required
  • Reinstatement: Unlimited timeframe
  • No outline of coverage or replacement form required
⏳ Claim Requirements
  • Death must occur within 90 days of covered accident
  • Claim must be submitted within 180 days of covered accident
🚫 Main Exclusions
  • Illness or disease — accident/AD&D only
  • Driving without valid license
  • ATVs, motor sports races, hang gliding, bungee jumping, ultra-light planes
  • Active military duty beyond 31 days
  • Self-inflicted injuries, illegal activities, war
  • Under influence of alcohol or drugs
⚡
Plan Enhancer (AME / AME with Riders)
AME only: GI · AME with riders: health questionnaire required
✅ Elegibilidad (Agent Sales Guide p.17)
  • AME Only (CA, CO, DC, GA, KS, MI, TN, VA): Primary 18–64 yrs · no riders available · no child-only plans
  • AME with riders — Primary: Birth–73 yrs · ends 85 yrs
  • CHS rider — Primary: 18–64 yrs · ends 64 yrs
  • SIP rider — Primary: 18–64 yrs · ends 64 yrs
  • Spouse: 18–64 yrs (AME); 14–73 yrs (AME with riders)
  • Dependent child: Birth–25 yrs · ends 26 yrs
  • AME Only: Guaranteed Issue (no questionnaire)
  • AME with riders: Requires health questionnaire
🚫 Key Limitations
  • CHS rider NOT available in IA, MO
  • SIP rider NOT available in ME, MT, ND
  • Multiple policies NOT allowed
  • Benefits require treatment within 30 days of accident
  • SIP rider: only pays for inpatient hospital admission due to sickness
  • AME claim: include Explanation of Benefits (EOB) from primary insurance
  • Reinstatement: 60 days after lapse (AME w&w/o Riders)
🎗️
Cancer & Cancer and Heart/Stroke (CHS)
GI for $25K/$30K · Questionnaire for $50K/$75K
✅ Elegibilidad (Agent Sales Guide p.17)
  • Cancer — Primary/Spouse: 18 yrs – 70 yrs · ends 75 yrs
  • Cancer — Dependent child: Birth – 25 yrs · ends 26 yrs
  • CHS — Primary/Spouse: 18 yrs – 64 yrs · ends 75 yrs
  • CHS — Dependent child: Birth – 25 yrs · ends 26 yrs
  • Cancer $25K/$30K options: Guaranteed Issue (no questionnaire)
  • Cancer $50K/$75K options: Requires health questionnaire
  • CHS $25K/$30K options: Guaranteed Issue (no questionnaire)
  • CHS $50K/$75K options: Requires health questionnaire
⏳ Waiting Periods
  • Cancer: 90-day waiting period from effective date
  • CHS (Heart/Stroke): 30-day waiting period
🚫 Main Exclusions
  • Cancer/CHS diagnosed BEFORE waiting period ends — no benefit
  • NSTEMI (non-ST-elevation heart attacks) NOT covered under CHS
  • Reinstatement: Cancer & CHS 180 days after lapse
💊
Term Life Critical Illness / Term Life
Requires health questionnaire · Ages 18–60
✅ Elegibilidad (Agent Sales Guide p.17)
  • CI — Primary/Spouse: 18 yrs – 60 yrs · ends 65 yrs
  • CI benefit: payable until End of term / 85 yrs
  • Term Life Only — Primary/Spouse: 18 yrs – 64 yrs · ends End of term or 85 yrs
  • Dependent child: N/A — no dependent coverage
  • Requires health questionnaire — NO guaranteed issue
  • No replacement allowed for Term Life Critical Illness
  • No outline of coverage required at point of sale
⏳ Waiting Periods
  • Cancer (Type A/B): 90-day waiting period
  • All other covered CI conditions: 30-day waiting period
🚫 Main Exclusions
  • NSTEMI (non-ST elevation heart attacks) specifically excluded
  • No dependent/child-only coverage
  • 25% partial benefit for: bypass, non-invasive cancer, Alzheimer's, heart valve surgery
  • CI benefit reduces total benefit — policy continues after CI payment
  • Reinstatement: 3 years after lapse (Term Life / Term Life with CI)
🦷
Select Dental PPO & Dental Indemnity
